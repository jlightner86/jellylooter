‚ùì FAQ

Why do I get "No space left on device"?
Docker volume mapping issue. Check that /storage points to your actual media storage. Run: docker exec jellylooter df -h /storage
Do I need to enable authentication?
No! Auth is now optional (off by default). Enable it in Settings if you want login protection.
Why aren't all items showing?
Pagination limits items per page. Increase "Items Per Page" in settings or use the page navigation.
How do I detect duplicates?
Configure your local Jellyfin server in Settings and click "Rebuild Cache". Items you own show a green badge.
